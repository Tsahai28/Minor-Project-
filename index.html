What you need to know

D'Equinoz is a cultural festival or college fest organised by the student community, involving participants from other colleges as well. Professional performing artists are also typically invited, and a number of competitions are held for students.and the fiesta begins on the 28th of February and keeps the adrenaline kicking till the 1st of March 2K20 so join us for a wonderful rondezvous of zest,ethos and amusement.

Important Dates:

Registration Dates: 20th of Jan 2020 to 25th of 2020.

Opening Ceremony: 28th of February 2020.

Closing Ceremony: 1st of March 2020.
