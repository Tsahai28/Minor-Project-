ACCOMODATION

POLICIES

1. The Accommodation for all students will be provided safely inside the campus hostels.
2. You are required to report at the Hospitality desk as soon as you arrive at the campus.
3. All students are required to carry their original college identity cards with photo proof to avail the accommodation and mess facilities.
4. Also they must follow the Rules and Regulations formulated by D'Equinox coordination committee.
5. Kindly note that the accommodation is strictly on a shared basis and any requests for individual rooms or any complaints in this regard shall not be entertained. 6. You will be provided mess facility in the campus itself. Apart from the regular mess facilities there is cafeteria and bakery in the campus
which can cater to your eating requirements.
7. College will not be responsible for any loss or damage to the belongings of students so please be careful if you are carrying something valuable.

FACILITIES PROVIDED

1. Accommodation from 28th Feb(evening) to 1st March 2020.
2. Mess facilities throughout your stay.
3. Mattresses and bedsheets will be provided to all the students who have confirmed accommodation. You need not stuff your backpacks with the same.

CHECKLIST

Please ensure you have the following items before coming to hospitality desk.
1, Hard copy of filled registration forms, with no spelling mistake.
2. Hard copy of NOC.
3. Two photographs of each student.
4. Original College identity cards for each participant.
